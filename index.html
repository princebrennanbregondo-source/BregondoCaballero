How to Create Your Wedding Invitation

Follow these simple steps to design a beautiful and personalized wedding invitation:

  1. Choose a Template: Click on one of the available template designs in the generator to start customizing.
  2. Enter Your Details: Fill in your names, event date, venue, address, and RSVP information in the input fields provided.
  3. Select Fonts & Colors: Use the font dropdown to pick your preferred style, toggle bold text if needed, and pick a color that matches your theme.
  4. Preview Your Invitation: Watch the live preview update in real-time as you make changes to see how your invitation will look.
  5. Download Your Invitation: Once you're happy with the design, click the "Download PNG" button to save your invitation image to your device.
  6. Print or Share: Use your downloaded PNG to print your invitations or share digitally with friends and family.
